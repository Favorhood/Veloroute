about

veloroute is a tool which can be used to evaluate proposed bike route alternatives. the application allows the user to add potential routes to the map, and compare them with the click of a button.

data assumptions

distance : distance data came from mapbox's directions api.


getting started

select the plus symbol to begin adding a new route to your analysis. click to add additional waypoints. markers can be adjusted to define your route. double click to end a route. routes cannot be adjusted after completion. for best results draw slowly . if no route is drawn between markers, try adjusting the markers .

once you have added all of the routes you care to analyze, click the blue chevron symbol to bring up the results. toggle between the raw data, standardized data, and elevation data tabs to view different summary data. it is possible to adjust the variable weights according to your perceived level of importance by selecting the dropdown marked "importance" on that standardized data tab.
